標題[爆卦] Twitter員工承認 右派言論易遭水桶
時間Fri Jan 12 21:12:09 2018
有一個秘密調查計畫叫做Project Veritas 訪問了許多前Twitter員工 他們透露，Twitter會用shadow banning（暗中停權）的方式來封殺右派言論 暗中停權的意思就是不告知用戶被停權了，用戶仍然能夠po文，但沒人看得見。 Twitter前員工說，停權的決定還是由管理員自行裁決 加州是個民主黨大本營，許多管理員都偏袒左派言論，封殺右派言論 他們看到自己不喜歡的言論，譬如說支持川普的發言，就會有偏見 然後用仇恨言論的名義水桶用戶 “Yeah, if they said this is: ‘Pro-Trump’ I don’t want it because it offends me, this, that. And I say I banned this whole thing, and it goes over here and they are like, ‘Oh you know what? I don’t like it too. You know what? Mo’s right, let’s go, let’s carry on, what’s next?'” “A lot of unwritten rules, and being that we’re in San Francisco, we’re in California, very liberal, a very blue state. You had to be… I mean as a company you can’t really say it because it would make you look bad, but behind closed doors are lots of rules.” “There was, I would say… Twitter was probably about 90% Anti-Trump, maybe 99% Anti-Trump.” 來源：https://goo.gl/BB5kCX -- http://imgur.com/OnzJ19N
