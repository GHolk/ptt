HODIE (adv) today VERO (adv) truly; really INFORTVNATA (a) INFORTVNATVS的主格單數陰性; unfortunate; unlucky SVM (v) I am Today i'm really unlucky... 今天我的運氣真差... 女：PESSIMVS CASVS, QVID FACIAM? U..Umm! ㄟ？有人來了嗎？嗯... 女：QVAEREBAM INSTRVMENTA GREGI MEO, SED VESTAE ALICVI ADHAESIT AC MOVERE NON POSSVM NE EAS SCINDANTUR!
