Baseball, Soccer Seasons Get Underway in Taiwan 棒球與足球季在台灣開始 前言：昨天中職率先世界關門開打，竟然意外成為全世界目光，已經沒有體育新聞可以報 的美國體育記者彷彿在汪洋中找到一條扶木，竟然開始寫中職新聞，不僅如此，就連台灣 足甲也在報....。 https://nyti.ms/3em7sRE TAIPEI — The baseball and soccer seasons got underway behind closed doors in Taiwan at the weekend, providing rare live action for fans at home at a time when the COVID-19 pandemic has shut down most professional sport around the globe. 臺北——棒球和足球賽季於週末在臺灣閉門進行，爲國內的球迷提供了罕見的現場表演， 而此時正值COVID-19爆發的致命流感使全球大多數職業體育運動陷入癱瘓。 Taiwan has been comparatively successful in containing the spread of the virus, reporting to date 388 cases and six deaths in a population of around 24 million. 臺灣在控制病毒傳播方面比較成功，迄今爲止報告了388例病例和6例死亡，臺灣人口約 2400萬。 Globally, more than 110,000 people have died from the virus. 全球已有11萬多人死於這種病毒。 The Chinese Professional Baseball League's much delayed start was pushed back by a further 24 hours after rain swept across the island, forcing the postponement of the traditional season-opener on Saturday. 中華職業棒球聯賽(Chinese Professional Baseball League)推遲已久的開賽時間又被推 遲了24小時，原因是臺灣遭遇暴雨襲擊，迫使原定於週六舉行的傳統賽季首場比賽被迫推 遲。 It therefore fell to Cuban lefthander Ariel Miranda, formerly of the Seattle Mariners, to throw the first pitch of the season for the Chinatrust Brothers against the Uni-President Lions at Taichung Intercontinental Baseball Stadium on Sunday. 因此，週日在臺中洲際棒球場(Taichung Intercontinental Baseball Stadium)，爲中信 金控兄弟(Chinatrust Brothers)和統一獅隊(Uni-President Lions)投出本賽季首戰 的球權落在了曾效力於西雅圖水手隊(Seattle Mariners)的古巴左投手阿里爾·米蘭 達(Ariel Miranda)身上。 Taiwan President Tsai Ing-wen posted a picture on her Facebook page on Sunday of her watching the baseball match at home, accompanied by one of her cats, and calling on everyone else to join her and watch from home. 週日，臺灣總統蔡英文在她的臉書主頁上發佈了一張她在家看棒球比賽的照片，旁邊是她 的一隻貓，並呼籲所有人都加入她的行列，在家觀看比賽。 "What's more important is once the epidemic is over, we'll see each other at the baseball pitch!" she wrote. 她寫道:“更重要的是，一旦疫情結束，我們就可以在棒球場上見面了!” No fans were allowed in the 20,000-seater stadium to watch the Lions win 4-1, with health concerns limiting attendees to a total of 200 players, coaches, umpires, cheerleaders and journalists, local media reported. 據當地媒體報道，由於擔心健康問題，只有200名球員、教練、裁判員、拉拉隊隊員和記 者才能在這座有2萬個座位的體育場觀看獅隊4:1獲勝的比賽。 Rakuten Monkeys had placed cardboard cut-outs of fans replete with face masks in the stands for the washed-out game on Saturday, while anyone entering the baseball stadiums had their temperatures checked. 樂天桃猿(Rakuten Monkeys)將裝滿面具的紙板做成的球迷雕像放在看臺上，以觀看週六 的泡湯的比賽，所有進入棒球場的人都要接受體溫檢測。 The fourth season of the Taiwan Football Premier League also got underway on Sunday with Tapei-based champions Tatung City FC 3-2 losing to their rivals from the southern city of Kaohsiung, Taipower, on the opening day. 台灣企業甲級足球聯賽第四個賽季也於週日開始，在開幕當天，以台北爲主場的冠軍大同 城市隊以3-2輸給了來自南部城市高雄的對手，台電隊(Taipower)。 Tatung have won the championship in all three seasons of the Taiwan Football Premier League, which runs from April to October, with Taipower runners-up each year. 大同在台灣企業甲級足球聯賽(Taiwan Football Premier League)的三個賽季中都獲得 了冠軍，其中臺灣電力(Taipower)是每年的亞軍。台灣企業甲級足球聯賽從4月持續到 10月。 （已經沒有東西可以報了，連足甲都再報，要是疫情再不過去，要報青棒了，很多美國
